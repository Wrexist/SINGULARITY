Frequently asked

Is there a cost? The full game is free. There are no ads. One optional Premium unlock adds quality-of-life and cosmetic extras — a longer offline-earnings cap, “Founder” status, and future hall themes. It’s a convenience, not pay-to-win: there’s nothing competitive to buy and you can’t purchase resources or progress directly.

Does it work offline? Yes. Your lab keeps earning while you’re away, up to the offline cap.

Where is my progress saved? On your device only. Deleting the app erases it; there’s no cloud account.

I restored / reinstalled — how do I get Premium back? Open Settings → Premium → Restore.

How do I reset my game? Settings → “reset save”. (This is permanent.)
