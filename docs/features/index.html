Features

Small systems, deep game

Three resources, one clean loop, and a world that visibly grows. Here’s everything you’ll play with.

The 2.5D hall

A data center that physically grows

Buy a rack and it slides in and powers on. Cooling, overclocking and automation all manifest on the floor. Tap the open edges to pour more slab — the room expands in that direction. No two labs look alike.

  • Parametric rendering — boxes, lights and glow, no image assets or bloat.
  • Era transitions re-skin the whole hall as your empire levels up.
  • Tap-to-expand floor with live cost markers.
Research tree

Climb an absurd AI tech tree

The progression spine. Branching research across every era, from backprop refactors to “mixture of experts” and beyond — with witty flavor and real trade-offs. Depth is revealed in waves, so you’re never buried.

  • Exclusive forks — pick Closed API or Open Weights and commit to a build.
  • Categories — Foundations, Efficiency, Scale, Product, Frontier.
Prestige loop

Ship the Model

The core reset. Ship your flagship model to bank permanent Legacy-Weight multipliers, then do it all again — faster. Choose how you ship: deploy commercially, open-source it, or sell to a hyperscaler. Each path plays differently.

  • Three ship modes with distinct rewards and downsides.
  • Legacy investments — specialise a lane between runs.
Risk & reward

Buy your data — legally, or not

Source training data from licensed vendors, or risk the dark-web Bazaar for cheaper bytes. Watch your Regulatory Heat — push your luck and a surprise audit might “request its cut.”

  • Poison & raid odds on shady batches — cheaper, but it can bite.
  • Lobby regulators to cool your Heat… for a price.
