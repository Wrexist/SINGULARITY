Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Singularity Inc. (“the app”) is a single-player, offline game. We designed it to collect nothing.

What we collect

No personal data is collected, stored on our servers, or transmitted anywhere. The app has no accounts, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress and settings are saved locally on your device only, using standard on-device storage. We never see this data. Deleting the app removes it.

Purchases

The optional Premium unlock is processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple’s handling of purchases is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. There is no chat, no user-generated content, and no links to unvetted external content.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
