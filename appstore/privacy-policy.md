# Privacy Policy — Singularity Inc.
*Last updated: 2026-06-24*

Singularity Inc. ("the app") is a single-player, offline game. We designed it to collect **nothing**.

## What we collect
**No personal data is collected, stored on our servers, or transmitted anywhere.** The app has no
accounts, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

## Data stored on your device
Your game progress and settings are saved **locally on your device only** (standard on-device
storage). We never see this data. Deleting the app removes it.

## Purchases
The optional Premium unlock is processed entirely by **Apple** via the App Store. We do not receive
or store your payment information. Apple's handling of purchases is governed by Apple's privacy
policy.

## Children
The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. There is no chat, no user-generated
content, and no external links to unvetted content.

## Changes
If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
